Simple Chicken & Vegetable Soup

  • 1 lb chicken breast or thighs (boneless, skinless), diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the chicken: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-6 minutes. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.
  2. Saut√© the onion and garlic: In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until the onion becomes translucent (about 3-4 minutes).
  3. Add the vegetables: Add the sliced carrots and diced potatoes to the pot. Stir everything together for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the broth and chicken: Pour in the chicken broth and return the cooked chicken to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is fully cooked
  5. Season and serve: Add salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley or thyme if desired. Serve hot!
simple chicken soup is warm, hearty, and perfect for a quick meal. Feel free to add other vegetables or pasta for more variety!
