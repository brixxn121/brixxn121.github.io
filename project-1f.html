ABOUT

Pennsylvania, known as the "Keystone State," is a rich tapestry of history, culture, and natural beauty. Located in the Northeastern United States, it was one of the original thirteen colonies and played a pivotal role in the founding of the nation. The state is home to iconic landmarks, including Independence Hall and the Liberty Bell in Philadelphia, as well as the picturesque landscapes of the Appalachian Mountains and the serene beauty of the Pocono Mountains.
